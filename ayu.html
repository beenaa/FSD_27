Tung Tung Tung Sahur" (often misspelled as "Thung Thung Sahur") is a phrase that originated from an Indonesian tradition of drumming to wake people for the pre-dawn meal during Ramadan, which has been popularized and evolved into an internet meme and song, particularly on platforms like TikTok, often featuring exaggerated, AI-generated characters and narratives. The phrase itself mimics the sound of a drum and refers to the meal consumed before the day's fast
